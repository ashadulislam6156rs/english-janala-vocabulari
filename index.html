Frequently Asked Questions

  1. 1. How can I start learning English on this website?
    You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
  2. 2. Is this website free to use?
    Yes, this website is completely free to use. You can access all basic features without any cost. However, some advanced resources may require a premium subscription.
  3. 3. Do I need to create an account?
    Creating an account is optional for browsing general information. However, to save your progress, access personalized features, and use advanced tools, you will need to create an account.
  4. 4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
    You can build your English vocabulary by reading articles, books, and newspapers regularly. Practice writing new words in sentences, use flashcards, and engage in conversations. Our website also provides vocabulary exercises and quizzes to help you improve faster.
  5. 5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
    Yes, we provide certificates for certain completed courses. After successfully finishing a course and passing the final assessment, you can download your certificate from your dashboard.
